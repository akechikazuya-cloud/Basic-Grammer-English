AUXILIARY VERBS QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
===================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE ("BE" IN PROGRESSIVE SENTENCES)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct auxiliary "be" form.

1. I ____ painting the fence today.
   a) am        b) do        c) have

2. He ____ cooking dinner right now.
   a) is        b) does      c) has

3. They ____ been rehearsing for the play all week.
   a) are       b) have      c) do


SECTION 2: MULTIPLE CHOICE ("BE" IN PASSIVE SENTENCES)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct auxiliary "be" form.

4. I ____ awarded a scholarship.
   a) was       b) did       c) have

5. This mural ____ been painted by a local artist.
   a) is        b) has       c) does

6. The package ____ delivered this morning.
   a) was       b) has       c) did


SECTION 3: MULTIPLE CHOICE ("DO" IN NEGATIVES)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct auxiliary "do" form.

   a) don't     b) doesn't   c) isn't

9. They ____ finish the race.

SECTION 4: MULTIPLE CHOICE ("DO" IN QUESTIONS)

    a) have      b) are       c) do


SECTION 6: MATCHING (AUXILIARY VERB USES)
------------------------------------------
Match each sentence on the left with its correct auxiliary use 
on the right.

  1. "I am reading a book."              a) Passive voice
  2. "It was written by her."            b) Negative sentence
  3. "I do not agree."                   c) Progressive tense
  4. "Do you agree?"                     d) Question
  5. "I have finished."                  e) Perfect tense
