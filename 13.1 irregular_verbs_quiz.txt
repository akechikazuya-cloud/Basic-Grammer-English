IRREGULAR VERBS QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
===================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (REGULAR VERBS)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct past tense form of each regular verb.

1. play
   a) played     b) plaid       c) plays

2. clean
   a) cleant     b) cleaned     c) cleans

3. dance
   a) dancied    b) danceed     c) danced

4. wash
   a) washed     b) wush        c) washt


SECTION 2: MULTIPLE CHOICE (FAMILY 1 - NO CHANGE)
----------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct past tense form. Remember, these verbs don't 
change at all!

5. put
   a) put        b) putted      c) puted

6. hurt
   a) hurted     b) hurt        c) hurten

7. quit
   a) quitted    b) quit        c) quat

8. cost
   a) costed     b) cost        c) cast


SECTION 3: MULTIPLE CHOICE (FAMILY 2 - TWIN FORM)
----------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct past tense form. Remember, the past and past 
participle look the same in this family!

9. sell
   a) selled     b) sold        c) sould

10. catch
    a) catched    b) caught      c) cauth

11. bring
    a) bringed    b) brang       c) brought

12. think
    a) thinked    b) thought     c) thoughted


SECTION 4: MULTIPLE CHOICE (FAMILY 3 - THREE DIFFERENT FACES)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct past participle form (the form used with 
"have/has/had").

13. I have already ____ that book.
    a) write      b) wrote       c) written

14. She has ____ her arm before.
    a) broke      b) broken      c) break

15. They had ____ the mountain by noon.
    a) rose       b) risen       c) rise

16. He has never ____ a horse.
    a) ridden     b) rode        c) ride


SECTION 5: MATCHING (VERB FAMILIES)
------------------------------------------

  3. written       c) Family 2 (twin form)
  4. let           d) Family 1 (no change)
  5. told          e) Family 2 (twin form)
