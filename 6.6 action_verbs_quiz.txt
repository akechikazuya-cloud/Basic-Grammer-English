ACTION VERBS QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
===================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (ADDING "S")
--------------------------------------------
Choose the correct verb form to complete each sentence.

1. He ____ to work every day.
   a) walk      b) walks      c) walking

2. I ____ tea every morning.
   a) drink     b) drinks     c) drinking

3. The dog ____ at strangers.
   a) bark      b) barking    c) barks

4. They ____ soccer on weekends.
   a) play      b) plays      c) playing

5. She ____ the piano beautifully.
   a) play      b) plays      c) playing

6. We ____ pizza on Fridays.
   a) order     b) orders     c) ordering

7. It ____ very fast.
   a) run       b) runs       c) running

8. You ____ a lot of books.
   a) read      b) reads      c) reading

SECTION 3: MATCHING (NEGATIVE HELPER WORDS)
------------------------------------------------
Match the subject on the left with the correct negative 
helper word on the right.

  1. He           a) do not
  2. I            b) does not
  3. They         c) did not (past)
  4. She          d) do not
  5. We           e) do not
  6. It (past)    f) does not


SECTION 4: SPOT THE CONTRACTION
------------------------------------
Choose the correct contraction for each negative sentence.

16. He does not cook dinner.
    a) He don't cook dinner.     b) He doesn't cook dinner.   c) He didn't cook dinner.

17. We do not like spicy food.
    a) We doesn't like spicy food.  b) We didn't like spicy food.  c) We don't like spicy food.

18. They did not finish the race.
    a) They don't finish the race.  b) They didn't finish the race.  c) They doesn't finish the race.

19. It does not fly at night.
    a) It doesn't fly at night.  b) It don't fly at night.  c) It didn't fly at night.
