Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (CHOOSE "ON," "AT," OR "IN")
------------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct preposition to complete each sentence.

1. I placed the vase ____ the windowsill.
   a) on        b) at        c) in

2. Let's meet ____ 3 p.m.
   a) on        b) at        c) in

3. I usually check my email ____ the morning.
   a) on        b) at        c) in

4. The mail arrives ____ Tuesdays.
   a) on        b) at        c) in

5. There's a concert ____ the town hall.
   a) on        b) at        c) in

6. My hometown is Austin, which is ____ Texas.
   a) on        b) at        c) in
