Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (CHOOSE "WITH," "OVER," OR "BY")
------------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct preposition to complete each sentence.

1. I ordered a burger ____ a side salad.
   a) with       b) over       c) by

2. Come ____ to our place for the game night.
   a) with        b) over       c) by

3. Can I stand ____ the window?
   a) with         b) over       c) by

4. The engine was repaired ____ a technician.
   a) with          b) over       c) by

5. I fixed the chair ____ a screwdriver.
   a) with           b) over       c) by

6. This bill is ____ our monthly budget.
   a) with            b) over       c) by
