GERUNDS QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
===================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (GERUND AS SUBJECT)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct gerund form to complete each sentence.

1. ____ is great exercise.
   a) Swim       b) Swimming      c) To swim

2. ____ takes a lot of patience.
   a) Cook       b) Cooking       c) To cook

3. ____ opens your mind to new cultures.
   a) Travel     b) Traveling     c) To travel


SECTION 2: MULTIPLE CHOICE (GERUND AFTER "BE" VERBS)
----------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct gerund form.

4. My favorite hobby is ____.
   a) draw       b) drawing       c) to draw

5. Her passion is ____.
   a) dance      b) dancing       c) to dance

6. His job is ____.
   a) teach      b) teaching      c) to teach


SECTION 3: MULTIPLE CHOICE (GERUND AFTER CERTAIN VERBS)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct gerund form.

7. She enjoys ____ on weekends.
   a) hike       b) hiking        c) to hike

8. He loves ____ landscapes.
   a) paint      b) painting      c) to paint

9. They finished ____ the house.
   a) clean      b) cleaning      c) to clean

10. We are considering ____ to a new city.
    a) move       b) moving        c) to move


SECTION 4: MULTIPLE CHOICE (GERUND AFTER A PREPOSITION)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct gerund form.

11. I improved my Spanish by ____ every night.
    a) practice   b) practicing    c) to practice

12. She is nervous about ____ in public.
    a) speak      b) speaking      c) to speak

13. Thank you for ____ me move.
    a) help       b) helping       c) to help

14. He is proud of ____ the race.
    a) finish     b) finishing     c) to finish


SECTION 5: MATCHING (GERUND ROLES)
------------------------------------------
Match each sentence on the left with the correct gerund role 
on the right.

  1. "Reading builds vocabulary."            a) After a preposition
  2. "My hobby is painting."                 b) Subject of the sentence
  3. "I enjoy cooking."                      c) After a "be" verb
  4. "She's good at singing."                d) After a certain verb


SECTION 6: TRUE OR FALSE (POSSESSIVE + GERUND)
------------------------------------------------------
Read each sentence. Circle TRUE if the possessive + gerund form is 
correct, or FALSE if it is wrong.

15. "I appreciate your helping me with this."     TRUE / FALSE
16. "Do you mind me open the window?"              TRUE / FALSE
17. "She was upset about his forgetting her birthday."   TRUE / FALSE
18. "Nobody noticed Maria's arriving late."          TRUE / FALSE
19. "You will be impressed by they organizing skills."   TRUE / FALSE


SECTION 7: MULTIPLE CHOICE (SPOT THE GERUND)
--------------------------------------------------
Choose the gerund in each sentence.

20. Swimming is my favorite summer activity.
    a) Swimming    b) is        c) favorite

21. I don't mind waiting a little longer.
    a) don't       b) mind      c) waiting

22. She is thinking about studying abroad.
    a) thinking    b) about     c) studying

23. Thank you for listening to my story.
    a) Thank       b) for       c) listening
