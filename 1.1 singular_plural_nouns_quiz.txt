# Singular and Plural Nouns — Practice Quiz

**Instructions:** Complete each section below. Try to answer without looking back at the rules first — then check your work with the answer key at the end.

---

## Part 1: Fill in the Plural (Regular "-s" and "-es" nouns)

Write the plural form of each word.

1. lamp → __________
2. brush → __________
3. dish → __________
4. car → __________
5. fox → __________
6. glass → __________
7. pencil → __________
8. bus → __________

---

## Part 2: F / FE Endings

Change these words to their plural form (remember: f/fe → ves).

1. wolf → __________
2. knife → __________
3. shelf → __________
4. leaf → __________
5. scarf → __________ *(tricky one — check if it follows the rule!)*

---

## Part 3: Irregular Plurals

Match each singular noun to its correct plural. Write the plural next to it.

1. tooth → __________
2. person → __________
3. foot → __________
4. mouse → __________
5. child → __________
6. goose → __________

---

## Part 4: Y and O Endings

Choose the correct plural form for each pair.

1. city → (citys / cities)
2. day → (days / daies)
3. hero → (heros / heroes)
4. photo → (photos / photoes)
5. baby → (babys / babies)
6. toy → (toys / toies)

---

## Part 5: Same Singular and Plural

Circle whether each sentence uses the noun correctly.

1. I saw three **sheep** in the field. ✅ / ❌
2. I saw three **deers** in the forest. ✅ / ❌
3. There are two new **series** on TV this year. ✅ / ❌
4. We caught five **fish** at the lake. ✅ / ❌

---

## Part 6: Fix the Mistake

Each sentence has one incorrect plural noun. Find it and write the correct form.

1. The two womans walked into the store. → __________
2. I have three knifes in the kitchen drawer. → __________
3. There are five boxs under the bed. → __________
4. My cousins have two babys. → __________
5. The farmer has ten sheeps. → __________

---
---

# Answer Key

### Part 1
1. lamps 2. brushes 3. dishes 4. cars 5. foxes 6. glasses 7. pencils 8. buses

### Part 2
1. wolves 2. knives 3. shelves 4. leaves 5. scarves *(note: "scarfs" is also accepted in modern usage)*

### Part 3
1. teeth 2. people 3. feet 4. mice 5. children 6. geese

### Part 4
1. cities 2. days 3. heroes 4. photos 5. babies 6. toys

### Part 5
1. ✅ 2. ❌ (should be "deer") 3. ✅ 4. ✅

### Part 6
1. women 2. knives 3. boxes 4. babies 5. sheep


---

**Tip for learners:** Keep a small notebook of "irregular" and "no fixed rule" nouns (like the o/y-ending words) as you meet new ones. These are the words worth reviewing often, since regular "-s" and "-es" nouns become automatic with practice.
