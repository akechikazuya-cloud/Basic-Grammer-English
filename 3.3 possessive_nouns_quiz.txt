POSSESSIVE NOUNS — PRACTICE QUIZ
=================================


PART 1: ADD THE APOSTROPHE
----------------------------
Rewrite each phrase correctly, adding the apostrophe (and s if needed).

 1. the cat toy               → ______________________________
 2. Marcus phone               → ______________________________
 3. the children playground    → ______________________________
 4. my parents anniversary     → ______________________________
 5. the boss office            → ______________________________
 6. the puppies food           → ______________________________
 7. James backpack             → ______________________________
 8. the women meeting room     → ______________________________


PART 2: CHOOSE THE CORRECT FORM
----------------------------------
Circle the correct possessive form.

 1. The (teacher's / teachers') desk is near the window. (one teacher)
 2. The (student's / students') lockers are down the hall. (many students)
 3. My (sister's / sisters') violin is broken. (one sister)
 4. The (dog's / dogs') bowls need water. (two dogs)
 5. (Chris's / Chris') laptop is on the table.
 6. The (men's / mens') jackets are on sale.


PART 3: ONE THING SHARED OR SEPARATE THINGS?
------------------------------------------------
Add 's correctly. Remember: shared thing = last name only;
separate things = both names.

 1. Emma and Noah ______ garden is beautiful. (they share one garden)
    → ______________________________

 2. Sara ______ and Tom ______ shoes were left by the door. (two different pairs)
    → ______________________________

 3. Mia and Leo ______ classroom was decorated for the party. (one shared classroom)
    → ______________________________

 4. Ana ______ and Jake ______ essays were both excellent. (two separate essays)
    → ______________________________


PART 4: FIX THE MISTAKE
--------------------------
Each sentence has one possessive error. Rewrite it correctly.

 1. The dogs' leash is red. (only one dog)
    → ______________________________________________

 2. My friends car is new. (one friend)
    → ______________________________________________

 3. The childrens' books are on the shelf.
    → ______________________________________________

 4. Tom and Jerrys' cartoon is funny. (one shared cartoon)
    → ______________________________________________

 5. Lisas and Kates bags are different colors. (separate bags)
    → ______________________________________________
