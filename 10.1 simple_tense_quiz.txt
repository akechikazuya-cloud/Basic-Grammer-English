SIMPLE TENSE QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
===================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (SIMPLE PRESENT)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct simple present form.

1. He ____ to school every day.
   a) walk       b) walks       c) walked

2. They ____ tea every morning.
   a) drink      b) drinks      c) drank

3. She ____ the piano on weekends.
   a) play       b) plays       c) played

4. We ____ pizza on Fridays.
   a) order      b) orders      c) ordered

5. I ____ my homework right after school.
   a) do         b) does        c) did


SECTION 2: MULTIPLE CHOICE (SIMPLE PAST)
----------------------------------------------
Choose the correct simple past form.

6. She ____ a letter yesterday.
   a) write      b) writes      c) wrote

7. We ____ to the beach last weekend.
   a) go         b) goes        c) went

8. He ____ his keys this morning.
   a) buy        b) bought      c) buys

9. I ____ dinner an hour ago.
   a) cook       b) cooked      c) cooks

10. They ____ the lesson yesterday.
    a) understand   b) understood   c) understands


SECTION 3: MULTIPLE CHOICE (SIMPLE FUTURE)
--------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct simple future form.

11. I ____ a marathon next year.
    a) run        b) will run       c) ran

12. She ____ dinner tomorrow night.
    a) will cook     b) cooks       c) cooked

13. We ____ groceries this weekend.
    a) will buy      b) buy         c) bought

14. He ____ Spanish next semester.
    a) will study    b) studies     c) studied


SECTION 4: MATCHING (REGULAR VS. IRREGULAR PAST)
------------------------------------------------------
Match the present-tense verb on the left with its correct 
simple past form on the right.

  1. walk         a) went
  2. go           b) walked
  3. buy          c) drank
  4. drink        d) bought
  5. clean        e) cleaned


SECTION 5: TRUE OR FALSE

15. "I will wrote a letter tomorrow."          TRUE / FALSE

17. "He drink coffee every morning."           TRUE / FALSE
18. "They will go to the gym tomorrow."        TRUE / FALSE
19. "We studied Spanish last year."            TRUE / FALSE
20. "I buys groceries every Friday."           TRUE / FALSE


SECTION 6: WHICH TENSE IS IT?
------------------------------------
Read each sentence and choose whether it is PRESENT, PAST, 
or FUTURE.

21. "I clean my room every weekend."
    a) present     b) past       c) future

22. "She wrote a letter last week."
    a) present     b) past       c) future

23. "We will buy groceries this weekend."
    a) present     b) past       c) future

    a) present     b) past       c) future

    a) present     b) past       c) future
