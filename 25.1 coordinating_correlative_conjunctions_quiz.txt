===================================
COORDINATING AND CORRELATIVE CONJUNCTIONS QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
===================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (CHOOSE THE CORRECT COORDINATING 
CONJUNCTION)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct conjunction to complete each sentence.

1. We visited a museum ____ a botanical garden.
   a) and       b) but       c) or

2. I love spicy food, ____ my sister can't handle it.
   a) so         b) but       c) nor

3. Would you like tea ____ coffee?
   a) and         b) or        c) yet

4. It started pouring rain, ____ we canceled the picnic.
   a) so          b) yet        c) for

5. She doesn't like coffee, ____ does she enjoy tea.
   a) and          b) but        c) nor

6. The exam was extremely difficult, ____ she passed with ease.
   a) so           b) yet         c) for


SECTION 2: MULTIPLE CHOICE (MEANING OF EACH CONJUNCTION)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct meaning or purpose of the conjunction used.

7. "I decided to stay home, for I was feeling under the weather."
   a) shows a result     b) means "because"     c) shows contrast

8. "He trains every day, yet he rarely wins competitions."
   a) shows a result      b) means "because"     c) shows unexpected contrast

9. "The restaurant had great reviews, so we decided to try it."
   a) shows a result       b) means "because"     c) shows contrast


SECTION 3: MATCHING (FANBOYS TO MEANING)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Match each coordinating conjunction on the left with its meaning 
on the right.

  1. And           a) shows a choice
  2. But            b) means "in addition to"
  3. Or              c) connects two negative ideas
  4. So              d) shows contrast/disagreement
  5. Nor              e) shows a result


SECTION 4: MULTIPLE CHOICE (CORRELATIVE CONJUNCTIONS)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct correlative pair to complete each sentence.

10. She excelled in ____ academics ____ sports.
    a) both / and      b) either / or      c) neither / nor

11. You can choose ____ the window seat ____ the aisle seat.
    a) both / and       b) either / or      c) not only / but also

12. He enjoys ____ hiking ____ camping.
    a) both / and        b) either / or      c) neither / nor

13. She earned top marks in ____ chemistry ____ physics.
    a) both / and         b) not only / but also   c) neither / nor


SECTION 5: MATCHING (CORRELATIVE PAIR TO PURPOSE)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Match each correlative pair on the left with its purpose 
on the right.

  1. Both/and              a) Rejects two options at once
  2. Either/or              b) Emphasizes two things equally
  3. Neither/nor             c) Presents two possible choices
  4. Not only/but also        d) Adds emphasis, highlighting two things
