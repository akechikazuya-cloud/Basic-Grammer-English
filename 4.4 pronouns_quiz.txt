PRONOUNS — PRACTICE QUIZ
============================


PART 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE
---------------------------
Circle the correct pronoun.

 1. Maria is a nurse. (She / Her) works at the city hospital.
 2. The boys forgot (their / them) lunch at home.
 3. Can you give the pen to (I / me)?
 4. (We / Us) are going to the concert tonight.
 5. The cat licked (itself / himself) clean.
 6. Please tell (they / them) about the meeting.
 7. (He / Him) and Lucas play soccer every weekend.
 8. This gift is for (she / her).
 9. The children made the cards (themselves / theirselves).
10. (I / Me) baked a cake for the party.


PART 2: MATCH THE PRONOUN TYPE
----------------------------------
Match each pronoun on the left with its correct category on the right.
Write the letter of the category next to each pronoun.

  1. himself         A. Singular Subject
  2. they             B. Singular Object
  3. her (as in "I saw her")   C. Plural Subject
  4. we               D. Plural Object
  5. it (as in "It is cold")  E. Singular Reflexive
  6. us               F. Plural Reflexive
  7. ourselves


PART 3: SUBJECT OR OBJECT?
------------------------------
Read each sentence. Circle whether the underlined pronoun is a
SUBJECT or an OBJECT. (The pronoun to focus on is in CAPITAL letters.)

 1. SHE called her sister yesterday.              SUBJECT / OBJECT
 2. The teacher gave THEM homework.                SUBJECT / OBJECT
 3. WE watched a movie last night.                 SUBJECT / OBJECT
 4. My uncle visited US last summer.               SUBJECT / OBJECT
 5. HE plays the guitar very well.                 SUBJECT / OBJECT
 6. The coach picked HIM for the team.             SUBJECT / OBJECT


PART 4: FILL IN THE BLANK (WORD BANK)
-----------------------------------------
Choose the correct pronoun from the word bank to complete each
sentence. Each word is used only once.

Word bank: herself | they | him | its | we

 1. The dog wagged ______ tail happily.
 2. Daniel forgot his jacket, so I gave it back to ______.
 3. ______ are planning a trip to Japan next month.
 4. Emma printed the report ______ before the meeting.
 5. ______ should leave soon, or we will be late.


PART 5: TRUE OR FALSE
--------------------------
Circle TRUE or FALSE for each statement.

 1. "It" can be used to refer to a person's name directly.       TRUE / FALSE
 2. "Myself" is an example of a reflexive pronoun.                TRUE / FALSE
 3. "Them" is a subject pronoun.                                  TRUE / FALSE
 4. "We" is used when the speaker includes other people.          TRUE / FALSE
 5. Reflexive pronouns are used when the subject and object       TRUE / FALSE
    refer to the same person.


PART 6: IDENTIFY THE ERROR
-------------------------------
Each sentence contains one incorrect pronoun. Circle the error.

 1. Me and my sister went to the park.
 2. The team celebrated theirselves after the win.
 3. Give the keys to she.
 4. Him is my best friend.
 5. The baby fed hisself with a spoon.
