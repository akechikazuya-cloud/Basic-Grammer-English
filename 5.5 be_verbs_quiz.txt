===================================
"BE" VERBS QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
===================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE
------------------------------
Choose the correct "be" verb to complete each sentence.

1. I ____ a nurse.
   a) is      b) am      c) are

2. She ____ my sister.
   a) am      b) is      c) are

3. They ____ at the beach.
   a) is      b) am      c) are

4. It ____ a cold morning.
   a) am      b) is      c) are

5. We ____ ready for the trip.
   a) am      b) is      c) are

6. You ____ very kind.
   a) am      b) is      c) are

7. He ____ not happy today.
   a) am      b) is      c) are

8. The cats ____ hungry.
   a) is      b) am      c) are


SECTION 2: TRUE OR FALSE
------------------------------
Read each sentence. Circle TRUE if the "be" verb is correct, 
or FALSE if it is wrong.

9.  "He are a doctor."          TRUE / FALSE
10. "I am a student."           TRUE / FALSE
11. "They is my cousins."       TRUE / FALSE
12. "We are on the bus."        TRUE / FALSE
13. "She am tired."             TRUE / FALSE
14. "It is a good movie."       TRUE / FALSE
15. "You are my friend."        TRUE / FALSE


SECTION 3: MATCHING
------------------------------
Match the subject on the left with the correct "be" verb on the right.
Write the matching letter next to each number.

  1. He          a) am
  2. I           b) are
  3. They        c) is
  4. We          d) is
  5. She         e) are
  6. It          f) are


SECTION 4: SPOT THE CONTRACTION
------------------------------
Choose the correct contraction for each negative sentence.

16. He is not ready.
    a) He amn't ready.      b) He isn't ready.      c) He aren't ready.

17. We are not late.
    a) We isn't late.       b) We amn't late.       c) We aren't late.

18. It is not raining.
    a) It isn't raining.    b) It aren't raining.   c) It amn't raining.

19. They are not tired.
    a) They isn't tired.    b) They aren't tired.   c) They amn't tired.


SECTION 5: QUESTION OR STATEMENT?
------------------------------
Look at the word order. Circle Q if it is a question, 
or S if it is a statement.

20. "Is she a teacher?"          Q / S
21. "He is a teacher."           Q / S
22. "Are you ready?"             Q / S
23. "We are ready."              Q / S
24. "Am I late?"                 Q / S


SECTION 6: PICK THE RIGHT QUESTION FORM
------------------------------
Choose the correctly formed question.

25. a) He is tired?         b) Is he tired?         c) Tired is he?
26. a) Are they home?       b) They are home?       c) Home are they?
