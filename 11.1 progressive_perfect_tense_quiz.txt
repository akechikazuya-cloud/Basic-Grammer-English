PROGRESSIVE AND PERFECT TENSE QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
=========================================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (PRESENT PROGRESSIVE)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct present progressive form.

1. I ____ dinner right now.
   a) cook       b) am cooking       c) cooked

2. She ____ a book at the moment.
   a) reads      b) is reading       c) read

3. They ____ soccer right now.
   a) play       b) are playing      c) played


SECTION 2: MULTIPLE CHOICE (PAST PROGRESSIVE)
--------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct past progressive form.

4. I ____ when the phone rang.
   a) was sleeping    b) sleep      c) slept

5. We ____ dinner when the power went out.
   a) were cooking    b) cook       c) cooked

6. He ____ his homework when I called.
   a) was doing       b) does       c) did


SECTION 3: MULTIPLE CHOICE (FUTURE PROGRESSIVE)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct future progressive form.

7. I ____ at this time tomorrow.
   a) will be studying    b) study      c) studied

8. She ____ when you arrive.
   a) will be painting    b) paints     c) painted

9. They ____ next Saturday afternoon.
   a) will be swimming    b) swim       c) swam


SECTION 4: MATCHING (PERFECT TENSE FORMULAS)
--------------------------------------------------
Match each tense on the left with its correct formula on the right.

  1. Present Perfect       a) will have + past participle
  2. Past Perfect          b) have/has + past participle
  3. Future Perfect        c) had + past participle


SECTION 5: MULTIPLE CHOICE (PRESENT PERFECT)
--------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct present perfect form.

10. I ____ that movie three times already.
    a) watch      b) have watched     c) watched

11. She ____ in this city for ten years.
    a) has lived     b) lives      c) lived

12. We ____ our homework already.
    a) finish        b) have finished   c) finished


SECTION 6: MULTIPLE CHOICE (PAST PERFECT)
------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct past perfect form.

13. I ____ the movie before it came to theaters.
    a) had seen      b) saw       c) see

14. She ____ French before she moved to Canada.
    a) had studied   b) studied   c) studies

15. They ____ dinner before the guests arrived.
    a) had cooked    b) cooked    c) cook


SECTION 7: MULTIPLE CHOICE (FUTURE PERFECT)
--------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct future perfect form.

16. I ____ a hundred laps by the end of training.
    a) will have swum    b) swim       c) swam

17. She ____ the book by Friday.
    a) will have read    b) reads      c) read

18. We ____ here for five years by next spring.
    a) will have lived   b) live       c) lived


SECTION 8: TRUE OR FALSE
------------------------------
Read each sentence. Circle TRUE if the tense form is correct, 
or FALSE if it is wrong.

19. "I am studying for my exam right now."          TRUE / FALSE
20. "She were reading when the phone rang."          TRUE / FALSE
21. "We have lived here for ten years."               TRUE / FALSE
22. "He had already left when I arrived."             TRUE / FALSE
23. "They will has finished by tomorrow."             TRUE / FALSE
24. "I will be studying at this time tomorrow."       TRUE / FALSE


SECTION 9: WHICH TENSE IS IT?
------------------------------------
Read each sentence and choose the correct tense name.

25. "I was painting when it started to rain."
    a) past progressive     b) future perfect     c) present perfect

26. "She has known him for years."
    a) past perfect          b) present perfect    c) future progressive

27. "We will have finished the project by June."
    a) future perfect        b) present progressive   c) past perfect

28. "They are swimming right now."
    a) present progressive   b) past progressive    c) future perfect
