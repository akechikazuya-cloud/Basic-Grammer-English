COMPARATIVE AND SUPERLATIVE ADJECTIVES QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
=================================================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (ONE-SYLLABLE ADJECTIVES)
----------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct comparative or superlative form.

1. This box is ____ than that one.
   a) small      b) smaller      c) smallest

2. Today is the ____ day of the year so far.
   a) hot        b) hotter       c) hottest

3. His car is ____ than mine.
   a) fast       b) faster       c) fastest

4. That is the ____ river in the country.
   a) long       b) longer       c) longest

5. This soup is ____ than the other one.
   a) cold       b) colder       c) coldest


SECTION 2: MULTIPLE CHOICE (ADJECTIVES ENDING IN "-Y")
-------------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct comparative or superlative form.

6. She is ____ than her sister.
   a) happy      b) happier      c) happiest

7. This is the ____ day of my life.
   a) busy       b) busier       c) busiest

8. My bag is ____ than yours.
   a) heavy      b) heavier      c) heaviest

9. That was the ____ joke I've ever heard.
   a) funny      b) funnier      c) funniest

10. This test is ____ than the last one.
    a) easy       b) easier       c) easiest


SECTION 3: MULTIPLE CHOICE (LONGER ADJECTIVES)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct comparative or superlative form.

11. This laptop is ____ than that one.
    a) expensiver      b) more expensive      c) most expensive

12. She is the ____ person I know.
    a) generouser      b) more generous       c) most generous

13. He is ____ than his coworker.
    a) more careful    b) carefuller          c) most careful

14. This is the ____ movie of the year.
    a) excitinger      b) more exciting       c) most exciting


SECTION 4: MATCHING (IRREGULAR ADJECTIVES)
------------------------------------------------
Match the adjective on the left with its correct superlative form 
on the right.

  1. good          a) worst
  2. bad           b) least
  3. little        c) best
  4. far           d) most
  5. much/many     e) furthest

SECTION 6: "THAN" OR "THE"?
--------------------------------
Choose which word correctly completes each sentence.

21. Maria is taller ____ her cousin.
    a) than       b) the

22. This is ____ oldest building in the city.
    a) than       b) the

23. My phone is newer ____ yours.
    a) than       b) the

24. That was ____ most delicious meal I've had all year.
    a) than       b) the

25. He runs faster ____ me.
    a) than       b) the
