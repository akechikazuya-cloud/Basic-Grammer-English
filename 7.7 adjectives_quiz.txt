ADJECTIVES QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
===================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (SPOT THE ADJECTIVE)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the adjective in each sentence.

1. The tall boy waved at us.
   a) boy       b) tall       c) waved

2. She wore a beautiful dress.
   a) wore      b) dress      c) beautiful

3. We saw a fierce storm last night.
   a) fierce    b) saw        c) storm

4. He has a quiet voice.
   a) has       b) quiet      c) voice

5. They live in a small village.
   a) live      b) small      c) village


SECTION 2: MATCHING (OPPOSITES WITH PREFIXES)
----------------------------------------------------
Match the adjective on the left with its correct opposite on the right.

  1. honest         a) inactive
  2. patient         b) unfair
  3. active          c) dishonest
  4. fair            d) impatient
  5. visible          e) invisible


SECTION 3: MULTIPLE CHOICE (CHOOSE THE RIGHT PREFIX)
----------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct opposite form of each word.

6. connect
   a) unconnect     b) disconnect     c) inconnect

7. formal
   a) unformal      b) informal       c) disformal

8. obedient
   a) unobedient    b) inobedient     c) disobedient

9. comfortable
   a) uncomfortable b) incomfortable  c) discomfortable

10. tolerant
    a) untolerant    b) intolerant     c) distolerant


SECTION 4: TRUE OR FALSE (ADJECTIVE ORDER)
------------------------------------------------
Read each phrase. Circle TRUE if the adjective order is correct, 
or FALSE if it is wrong.

11. "A red small French car"           TRUE / FALSE
12. "A small old red French car"       TRUE / FALSE
13. "A wooden old round table"         TRUE / FALSE
14. "A round old wooden table"         TRUE / FALSE
15. "Spicy Mexican street tacos"       TRUE / FALSE
16. "Mexican spicy street tacos"       TRUE / FALSE


SECTION 5: PUT THEM IN ORDER
------------------------------
For each set of adjectives, choose the option with the correct order.

17. (Italian / leather / brown / large)
    a) large brown Italian leather      
    b) Italian large brown leather      
    c) leather large brown Italian

18. (old / silver / round / tiny)
    a) round old silver tiny
    b) tiny old round silver
    c) silver tiny round old

19. (wooden / ancient / Roman / long)
    a) long ancient Roman wooden
    b) ancient long Roman wooden
    c) wooden long ancient Roman


SECTION 6: "THE + ADJECTIVE" AS A GROUP OF PEOPLE
--------------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct meaning of each phrase.

20. "the elderly" refers to:
    a) one old person       b) elderly people in general       c) a young person

21. "the unemployed" refers to:
    a) people without jobs in general   b) one person with a job   c) rich people

22. "the injured" refers to:
    a) healthy people in general   b) injured people in general   c) one doctor


SECTION 7: TRUE OR FALSE (GENERAL RULES)
------------------------------------------
23. Adjectives usually come before the noun in English.     TRUE / FALSE
24. Adjectives always come after the noun in English.        TRUE / FALSE
25. "The rich" can mean "rich people in general."             TRUE / FALSE
