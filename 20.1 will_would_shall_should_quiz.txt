===================================
WILL/WOULD AND SHALL/SHOULD QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
===================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE ("WILL" USES)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct use of "will" described for each sentence.

1. "It will snow later this evening."
   a) willingness      b) prediction of the future     c) capacity

2. "This tank will hold fifty gallons of water."
   a) capacity/ability   b) determination     c) prediction

3. "I will finish this marathon no matter what."
   a) determination     b) capacity     c) willingness

4. "Will you please lower your voice?"
   a) prediction        b) willingness/consent     c) determination


SECTION 2: MULTIPLE CHOICE ("WOULD" USES)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct use of "would" described for each sentence.

5. "I would rather stay home tonight."
   a) preference         b) past habit     c) wish

6. "When I was a kid, we would visit my grandmother every summer."
   a) preference          b) past habit     c) possibility

7. "If I were the manager, I would change the schedule."
   a) wish                b) possibility     c) past habit

8. "I would love to visit Japan someday."
   a) wish                 b) preference     c) past habit


SECTION 3: MULTIPLE CHOICE ("SHALL" USES)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct form to complete each sentence.

9. ____ we get started?
   a) Will       b) Shall       c) Should

10. All employees ____ follow the safety guidelines.
    a) will        b) shall       c) would

11. Let's take a break, ____ we?
    a) shall       b) would       c) should


SECTION 4: MULTIPLE CHOICE ("SHOULD" USES)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the correct use of "should" described for each sentence.

12. "You should drink more water every day."
    a) advice        b) regret       c) expectation

13. "I should have studied more before the exam."
    a) advice         b) regret       c) opinion request

14. "The package should arrive by Friday."
    a) expectation     b) regret       c) advice

15. "What should we order for dinner?"
    a) advice          b) asking for opinion     c) expectation


SECTION 5: MATCHING (MODAL VERB TO MAIN USE)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Match each modal verb on the left with its most common use 
on the right.

  1. Will          a) Regret about a missed opportunity
  2. Would         b) Talking about the future
  3. Shall         c) Should have + past participle
  4. Should have    d) Polite suggestion or formal rule
  5. Should         e) Preference, wish, or past habit


SECTION 6: TRUE OR FALSE
------------------------------
Read each sentence. Circle TRUE if the modal verb form is correct, 
or FALSE if it is wrong.

16. "I will you tomorrow."                          TRUE / FALSE
17. "You should have tried the dessert."              TRUE / FALSE
18. "She would rather stay quiet than argue."          TRUE / FALSE
19. "Shall we dance?"                                  TRUE / FALSE
20. "There shall be no trespassing on this property."   TRUE / FALSE
21. "I would to visit Japan."                          TRUE / FALSE


SECTION 7: WHICH WORD FITS BEST?
------------------------------------------
Choose the modal verb that best completes each sentence.

22. If it rained, we ____ cancel the picnic.
    a) will       b) would       c) shall

23. ____ I open a window?
    a) Will        b) Would       c) Shall

24. Everyone ____ be here by 6 PM.
    a) will        b) should      c) shall

25. This suitcase ____ fit all my clothes for the trip.
    a) will        b) should      c) would
