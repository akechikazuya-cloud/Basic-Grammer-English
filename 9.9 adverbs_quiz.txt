ADVERBS QUIZ - PRACTICE EXERCISES
===================================
Instructions: All questions below are multiple choice, true/false, or 
matching. Just circle, underline, or write the letter/number of your 
answer. No full sentences needed!


SECTION 1: MULTIPLE CHOICE (SPOT THE ADVERB)
----------------------------------------------------
Choose the adverb in each sentence.

1. He speaks softly.
   a) He        b) speaks       c) softly

2. She finished her homework quickly.
   a) finished  b) homework     c) quickly

3. The movie was surprisingly good.
   a) movie     b) surprisingly c) good

4. They arrived late.
   a) They      b) arrived      c) late

5. I clean my room rarely.
   a) I         b) clean        c) rarely


SECTION 2: WHAT DOES THE ADVERB MODIFY?
----------------------------------------------

   a) verb      b) adjective    c) another adverb
